Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer Standard (5-7 business days), Expedited (2-3 business days), and Express (1 business day) shipping. Costs vary based on location and order weight, calculated at checkout. Orders over $100 qualify for free Standard shipping within the continental US.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unworn, undamaged shoes in their original packaging within 30 days of delivery. Please visit our Returns Center page to initiate a return. Some exclusions may apply to final sale items.

How do I know what size to order?

We recommend checking the specific brand's sizing chart, usually available on the product page. General size conversion charts (US, UK, EU) are also available in our Size Guide section. If you're between sizes, consider the shoe type (e.g., running shoes often benefit from sizing up slightly).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Shoe Deals gift cards.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive an email confirmation containing a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can use this information to monitor your package's progress.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we primarily ship within the United States. We are working on expanding our international shipping options. Please check back later or contact customer service for specific inquiries.
